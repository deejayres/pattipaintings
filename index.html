About Me

As an artist, I am influenced by the beauty of the movement of watercolor paint and the spontaneity achieved by its unexpected meanderings across the paper. Inspired by the bold colors of South Florida, I seek to portray its laid back attitude and tropical beauty. I am available for commissions and custom work. Please inquire via the form.
